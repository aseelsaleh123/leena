Black Wok Restaurant


Black Wok Restaurant - located in Nablus Rafidia Street, Chamber of Commerce Building, opposite the Academy, provides delicious food of various types.

The Black Wok Restaurant branch was recently opened in the city of Nablus, and has since become one of the most famous Asian restaurants there

, offering a variety of distinctive Chinese dishes such as sushi, pani puri, egg rolls, and ramen, in addition to a number of distinctive healthy salads.
